Faculty Course Mapping & D2L Utility

Automating LMS setup and improving cross-campus communication

Overview

This Python-based utility streamlines post-scheduling administrative tasks by generating reports that map faculty assignments across campuses and courses, and automates the creation of upload-ready files for populating Desire2Learn (D2L) course templates. Designed to reduce redundancy and enable precise communication, this tool addresses critical inefficiencies in cross-campus coordination and LMS setup.

Key Goals

  • Identify which faculty are teaching at which campuses (Alpharetta, Online, Clarkston, etc.)
  • Track who is teaching each individual course (e.g., MATH 1001, MATH 1111)
  • Generate reverse lookups: given a campus, what courses are being taught and by whom
  • Support targeted communication (e.g., emailing all instructors of a specific course)
  • Create upload-ready .txt files for bulk addition of faculty to shared D2L course templates
